The art of giving is a website aimed at helping non governmental organizations (NGO) by providing them with the required product collected from the donors. This website works as an interconnection between donors and required institution.The main motive of this website is to help the general public take more and more interest in donating and be able to experience the joy of helping. Through this platform we can donate any spare product that lying there in our house tasting dustby just proving the details of the product on the website and it will be collected from your doorstep and be delivered to the required NGO.
