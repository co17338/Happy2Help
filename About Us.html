About Us

We students of chandigarh college of engineering and technology namely Monarch Hasija,Gurdaman Singh and Agampreet Kaur have designed this platform to help ngo directly connect with the donators. This website helps provide a direct link between the donators and the NGOs.Our intiative aims to provide help and supply to those in need and also help our users experience the joy of helping others. Through this website you can donate any spare product like clothes,footwear,bags etc by just giving the product details and they will be collected from your doorstep and given to the required NGO.
